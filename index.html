Geographic Data Science

IMPORTANT: this is the repository for the 2017 version of the GDS course. A more recent (and recommended) one is available at:

darribas.org/gds18

Welcome to Geographic Data Science, a course taught by Dr. Dani Arribas-Bel in the Autumn of 2017 at the University of Liverpool.

The timetable for the course is:

  • Lectures: Mondays 9:00am-10:00am, CTH-LTC
  • Computer Labs: Mondays 11:00am-1:00pm, ENG-HHTC

Locations

  • CTH-LTC: Central Teaching Hub - Lecture Theatre C [Map]
  • ENG-HHTC: Harrison Hughes Building (Engineering), Computer lab (top floor) [Map]

Contact

Dani Arribas-Bel - D.Arribas-Bel [at] liverpool.ac.uk Lecturer in Geographic Data Science Office 508, Roxby Building, University of Liverpool - 74 Bedford St S, Liverpool, L69 7ZT, United Kingdom.
